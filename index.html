About me

Each flavor of ice cream is made from our liquid Nitrogen machine. You can see each ice cream flavor made right in front of you! We make it by putting on gloves and goggles first. Then pour a small amount of liquid nitrogen directly into the bowl with the ice cream ingredients. Continue to stir the ice cream, while slowly adding more liquid nitrogen. As soon as the cream base starts to thicken, add favorite topping.
